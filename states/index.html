Default Policy

This is a default policy of every state of the union. If the state page specifies the policy, it adds onto the default one.
  1. Angulator Policy:
    • The Zeionian State is allowed to hold elections between every 2 weeks and 4 months.
    • The elected Angulator is allowed to abdicate only 3 days after they have been elected.
    • Read More
  2. Modify State's policy:
    • The Zeionian State is allowed to modify its own policy by making a vote with their people and specifying exactly what they want to modify.
    • The poll must have a duration of 24 hours and 3 options: Yes, No, and Abstain.
    • The vote also must have a yes majority to be able to modify it.
  3. Military Policy:
    • The Zeionian State is required to have its own army and military, however they also must participate in the Zeion Union's Military.
    • The poll must have a duration of 24 hours and 3 options: Yes, No, and Abstain.
    • The vote also must have a yes majority to be able to modify it.
  4. Diplomatic Policy:
    • The Zeionian State allowed to make an alliance with any foreign power, as long as the union government allows it to.
    • The state is not allowed to declare war on any foreign power.
    • If the union is at war with a foreign power, the state must use its military and attack.
    • The state is allowed to back out by creating a poll to stay neutral, however the union government is allowed to veto the decision.
    • The vote also must have a yes majority to be able to stay neutral.
  5. Security Policy:
    • The Zeionian State must have security on its borders. Whenever a user joins a server, the staff must verify them for them to be able to interact with the rest of the server.
    • The state also must not have any role with administrator on it, and if it does, it shall not give randoms administrator or high positions.
