Policies

  1. Appointing Emperor:
    • The Zeionian State of Zelkad is allowed to appoint emperors for the union.
  2. Giving out State Titles:
    • The Zeionian State of Zelkad is allowed to give State Titles to any state of the union including Capital.
  3. Union Government:
    • The Zeionian State of Zelkad's government will be considered as the Union Government.
  4. Diplomacy:
    • The All allies of Zelkad would automatically be allies of the union, and all enemies of zelkad will be enemies of the union.
    • Only Zelkad is permitted to declare war on other nations. If it is at war, it will be considered as the union are at are
  5. Policy editing:
    • Zelkad is allowed to change the policy of any state in the union, via vote.
