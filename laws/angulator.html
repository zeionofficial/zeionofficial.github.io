Angulator law

  1. Constitutional Integrity
    • Angulators shall not modify The Constitution of The Phi. Only The Emperor of The Phi has the authority for such actions.
  2. Fairness in Punishment
    • Angulators shall not punish innocent inhabitants of The Phi, or punish them wrongfully. The individual can send a report to Emperor Steve to remove The Angulator.
  3. State Preservation
    • Angulators cannot add or remove any state from The Phi. Only The Emperor has such authority to add states into The Phi and remove them from The Phi.
  4. Zeption:
    • Angulators cannot claim property of The Phi that's not their such as The Supreme Flag, The Communist Flag, Icons, Names, etc. Only the creators of the resources and The Phi itself can claim the resources.
