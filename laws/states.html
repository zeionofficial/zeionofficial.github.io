State policy

  1. State name:
    • All states of The Phi shall have an official name in The Phi's States Database
    • The name of the State shall start with ze, za, or zo. The state shall not change its formal name into the State Name if they do not wish for so.
  2. Foreign integration:
    • States shall not join foreign powers as their memberstate, colony, or others. States shall only be a state of The Phi. If a state wishes to be apart of another Foreign Power, they shall ask The Emperor of The Phi to proceed, however The Emperor of The Phi shall be able to veto the State joining the Foreign Power at any time, even after the State already had joined.
